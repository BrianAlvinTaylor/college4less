How it works for your family

Simple steps, real results—designed to fit busy schedules.

1) Download a free app

Pick a subject that fits your student’s interests or degree goals. Starting is free and takes minutes.

2) Encourage 30 minutes/day

Short lessons, notecards, and quizzes recycle missed questions until mastered—steady, stress-light progress.

3) Earn credits that count

Schedule the CLEP exam, send scores, and apply credits where accepted. Repeat to multiply savings.

1 CLEP → 3 credits One class your student never has to take.
2 CLEPs → ≈ $3,000 saved Savings vary by school and course.
4 CLEPs → a semester sooner ~12 credits at many schools.

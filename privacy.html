College4Less Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how College4Less (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application, College4Less: U.S. History II Prep.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or contact details) through the College4Less app.

However, we use third-party services, including Google AdMob, that may collect data automatically to provide personalized or contextual advertisements. This data may include:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Advertising identifiers (such as IDFA or AAID)
  • General app usage data
  • Location data (approximate, not precise)

Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to our app and/or other sites on the Internet.

You can learn more about how Google uses data in its partners' sites and apps by visiting: Google’s Advertising Policy.

User Controls

You can control and limit the use of tracking technologies by adjusting the settings on your mobile device. Most devices allow you to reset your advertising identifier and opt out of personalized ads.

Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for general educational use and is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted to this page with an updated revision date.

FAQ for students

Quick answers to the questions we hear most.

Which colleges accept CLEP?

Thousands. Check your target school’s CLEP policy and required scores; most list which exams count for which classes.

How much time does prep take?

Many students prep ~30 minutes/day for ~30 days per exam. Your pace may vary—dashboards help you track readiness.

What makes College4Less different?

Mobile-first CLEP prep with short lessons, notecards, quizzes, and automatic recycling of missed questions by topic and globally.

Can I take multiple CLEPs?

Yes. Start with one subject to build confidence, then stack exams that fit your degree plan to maximize time and tuition savings.

Ready to earn your first 3 credits?

Pick a subject you like, study smart for 30 minutes a day, and turn effort into real college credit.

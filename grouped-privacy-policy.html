Grouped Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Grouped collects, uses, and discloses information when you use the Grouped mobile application.

Information We Collect

Grouped is a private photo-sharing app. When you use the app, you may create photo groups, join groups, upload photos, and share photos with people in those groups.

The app may use information needed to provide these features, including group names, group codes, display names, uploaded photos, photo metadata needed for app functionality, and related app activity.

Cloud Storage and Sharing

Grouped uses Apple iCloud and CloudKit to store and sync app content and support private group sharing. Content shared in a group may be visible to users who have joined that group.

Advertising

Grouped uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. Google AdMob may collect data automatically to provide personalized or contextual ads. This data may include device information, advertising identifiers, app interaction data, diagnostics, and approximate location.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services by visiting: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Tracking Controls

You can control app tracking permission through your iOS device settings. You may also limit or reset advertising identifiers through your device settings.

Children’s Privacy

Grouped is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with an updated effective date.

How it works in your school

Plug-and-play steps to launch and scale with minimal lift.

1) Share the free apps

Start with the five free 9–12 apps. Host QR codes in counseling offices, classrooms, and newsletters.

2) Set a routine

Encourage ~30 minutes/day. Use advisory, study hall, or after-school time. Track wins on the in-app dashboards.

3) Celebrate credits

Schedule CLEP exams, send scores, and award recognition for every pass. Use early wins to recruit the next cohort.

1 CLEP → 3 credits One class students never have to take.
2 CLEPs → ≈ $3,000 saved Savings vary by school and course.
4 CLEPs → a semester sooner ~12 credits at many schools.

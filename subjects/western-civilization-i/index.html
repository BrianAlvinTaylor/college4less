Western Civilization I
Ancient Near East to 1648

This subject explores the major political, religious, intellectual, cultural, and economic developments in Western civilization from ancient Mesopotamia to the mid-17th century. Students will examine key movements, ideas, and institutions that shaped Europe and its legacy.

  • Ancient Near East
  • Ancient Greece and Hellenistic Civilization
  • Ancient Rome
  • Medieval History
  • Renaissance and Reformation
  • Early Modern Europe, 1560–1648

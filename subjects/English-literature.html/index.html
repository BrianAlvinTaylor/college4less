English Literature
Survey of British and Postcolonial Literary Tradition

This subject explores the rich tradition of English literature from its Anglo-Saxon roots to the 21st century. Students will learn to analyze and appreciate major works of poetry, prose, and drama while gaining familiarity with key authors, literary forms, and movements. The exam emphasizes critical reading, interpretation, and historical context.

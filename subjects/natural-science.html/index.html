Natural Science
Explore the Living and Physical World

This course surveys foundational topics in biology, chemistry, physics, Earth science, and astronomy. Designed for non-science majors, it focuses on understanding scientific principles and applying them to real-world contexts. Emphasis is placed on interpreting data, understanding systems, and evaluating science in society.

  • Origin and Evolution of Life
  • Cell Structure, Division, and Genetics
  • Energy Transfer and Biosynthesis in Cells
  • Structure and Function in Organisms; Heredity
  • Ecology and Population Biology
  • Atomic Structure and Nuclear Reactions
  • Chemical Properties and Reactions
  • Energy, Motion, and Thermodynamics
  • Waves, Light, and Electricity
  • Earth and Space Science

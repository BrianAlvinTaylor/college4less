Chemistry
Structure, Reactions, and Principles of General Chemistry

This course prepares students to understand the fundamental principles of chemistry as covered in a typical one-year college-level general chemistry course. Topics include matter and bonding, reaction types, equilibrium, thermodynamics, and lab techniques.

  • Atomic Structure and Periodicity
  • Chemical Bonding
  • States of Matter: Gases, Liquids, and Solids
  • Solutions and Colligative Properties
  • Reaction Types and Redox Chemistry
  • Stoichiometry and Balancing Equations
  • Chemical Equilibrium
  • Chemical Kinetics
  • Thermodynamics
  • Descriptive Chemistry and Nomenclature
  • Experimental Chemistry and Lab Techniques

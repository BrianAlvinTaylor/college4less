Humanities
Literature, Art, Music, and Culture Across Civilizations

The Humanities subject explores the broad spectrum of human expression—from literature and philosophy to the visual and performing arts. It emphasizes critical appreciation of major works, movements, and cultural trends across historical periods and global traditions. This study will help you interpret, compare, and understand the rich tapestry of the human experience through art, drama, architecture, and thought.

Principles of Macroeconomics
Understand how the economy functions at a national and global scale

This subject introduces key macroeconomic principles, models, and tools used to analyze national income, inflation, unemployment, and policy. Students will explore aggregate demand and supply, fiscal and monetary policy, economic growth, and international finance through real-world scenarios and simplified models.

  • Basic Economic Concepts
  • Measurement of Economic Performance
  • National Income and Price Determination
  • Financial Sector
  • Inflation, Unemployment, and Stabilization Policies
  • Economic Growth and Productivity
  • Open Economy: International Finance

Introduction to Educational Psychology
Explore how students learn and how educators teach

This subject examines how psychological concepts apply to teaching and learning. You will explore cognitive and behavioral theories, student development, motivation, assessment, and individual differences — all within the context of the classroom. Mastery of these topics prepares you to evaluate teaching practices and support effective learning environments.

  • Behavioral Perspective

  • Testing and Assessment
  • Pedagogy and Instruction

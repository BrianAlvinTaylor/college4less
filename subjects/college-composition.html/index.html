College Composition
Mastering Academic Writing and Essay Development

This subject helps students develop the core skills needed to write effectively at the college level. It includes strategies for constructing clear arguments, analyzing sources, revising drafts, and writing in a variety of academic contexts. Each topic includes practical guidance, essay tips, and examples to help students prepare for the CLEP College Composition exam and beyond.

  • Conventions of Standard Written English
  • Revision and Editing Skills
  • Rhetorical Analysis
  • Building an Argument and Organizing an Essay
  • Thesis Statements and Topic Sentences
  • Using and Citing Source Materials
  • Research and Reference Skills
  • Types of Academic Essays
  • Synthesis Essay Strategies
  • Essay Practice Prompts and Sample Responses

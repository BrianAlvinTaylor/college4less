Principles of Marketing
E‑commerce and Digital Marketing

Overview

Digital channels enable brands to reach global audiences 24/7, personalize offers at scale, and measure results in real time. Successful e‑commerce integrates seamless user experience, data‑driven promotion, and omnichannel fulfillment.

Key Concepts

  • Online Buyer Journey: Awareness → Consideration → Conversion → Retention; mapped through web analytics.
  • Traffic Acquisition: Search engine optimization (SEO), pay‑per‑click (PPC), social media ads, influencer partnerships.
  • Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO): A/B testing headlines, CTAs, checkout flow to boost % of visitors who buy.
  • Email & Marketing Automation: Triggered flows—welcome, cart abandonment, win‑back—powered by customer data platforms (CDPs).
  • Content Marketing: Blogs, videos, UGC to build authority and organic reach.
  • Mobile Commerce (m‑commerce): Responsive design, app‑based loyalty, mobile wallets.
  • Omnichannel Fulfillment: Buy Online Pickup In‑Store (BOPIS), last‑mile delivery, returns logistics.
  • Analytics & KPIs: CAC, CLV, ROAS, bounce rate, average order value (AOV).
  • Privacy & Security: GDPR/CCPA compliance, SSL, PCI‑DSS, cookieless future strategies.

Step‑by‑Step Example

Scenario: ArtisanHome, a local decor shop, wants to sell nationwide via Shopify.

1 Platform Setup: Choose Shopify Basic; integrate Stripe & PayPal; enable Shop Pay.

2 SEO Foundation: Keyword research—"handmade ceramic vases"—optimize product titles, meta descriptions, alt tags.

3 Launch Campaign: Allocate $2 k to Facebook/Instagram look‑alike ads; use UTM parameters for tracking.

4 CRO: Install heat‑map tool; A/B test free‑shipping threshold ($50 vs. $75).

5 Automation: Set Klaviyo flow—abandoned cart email after 1 hr with 10 % coupon.

6 Measure: Target Month‑1 KPIs: 2 % conversion, CAC ≤ $30, AOV ≥ $60.

Final Answer: A holistic digital strategy—platform setup, traffic, CRO, automation, analytics—positions ArtisanHome for profitable national growth.

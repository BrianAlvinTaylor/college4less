U.S. History I
1500–1877 | Foundations of the American Nation

This course explores political, social, economic, and cultural developments in U.S. history from 1500 to 1877. It is divided into two major time periods, each covering five core themes.

1500–1789

  • Political Institutions, Political Developments, and Public Policy
  • Social Developments
  • Economic Developments
  • Cultural and Intellectual Developments
  • Diplomacy and Transnational Interactions

1790–1877

  • Political Institutions, Political Developments, and Public Policy
  • Social Developments
  • Economic Developments
  • Cultural and Intellectual Developments
  • Diplomacy and Transnational Interactions

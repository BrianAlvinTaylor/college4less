Principles of Management
Fundamentals of Leading and Organizing

This course surveys essential managerial functions—planning, organizing, leading, and controlling—along with human‑resource practices and global contemporary issues. Each topic aligns with CLEP® Principles of Management objectives.

  • Introduction to Management
  • Planning and Strategic Analysis
  • Organizing and Organizational Structure
  • Leadership and Motivation
  • Controlling and Quality Management
  • Human Resource Management
  • Decision Making and Problem Solving
  • Operations and Productivity
  • International Management and Ethics
  • Creativity, Innovation, and Change

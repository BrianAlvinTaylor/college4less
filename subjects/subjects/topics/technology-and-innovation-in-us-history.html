Technology and Innovation in U.S. History

Tracing the role of invention, industry, and information in shaping the nation

Overview

Technology and innovation have driven U.S. economic growth, social change, and global influence. From the industrial revolution to the digital age, American inventors and entrepreneurs have transformed how people work, live, and connect.

Key Developments and Eras

  • 19th-century industrialization: railroads, telegraph, steel, electricity
  • 20th-century advancements: automobiles, aviation, radio, nuclear energy
  • Space Race and Cold War technology
  • Rise of Silicon Valley and the tech economy
  • Internet, smartphones, and digital innovation
  • Biotech, renewable energy, and artificial intelligence in the 21st century

Trends in the History of Women and the Family

Examining the evolving roles of women and family structures in American history

Overview

Women's roles and family dynamics have shifted dramatically over U.S. history, reflecting broader economic, political, and social transformations. From suffrage and workforce participation to changing family norms, these trends reveal ongoing struggles and achievements in gender equality and domestic life.

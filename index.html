Common questions

Quick answers to get you started.

Do colleges really accept CLEP credits?
Thousands of accredited colleges accept CLEP. Always check your target school’s policy.
Is CLEP only for “top students”?
No. With focused prep and practice, most motivated students can pass and earn credit.
How much can I save?
Many exams award ~3 credits. At typical tuition rates, savings often exceed $1,500 per exam.
Which apps are free vs. paid?
Five iOS apps are free (US History I, US History II, Principles of Marketing, Natural Sciences, Humanities). Biology is a premium app.

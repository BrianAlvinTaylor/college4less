Common questions

Quick answers to help you decide if CLEP is right for you.

Do colleges really accept CLEP credits?
Yes. Thousands of accredited colleges nationwide accept CLEP credits. Always confirm with your school.
Is CLEP only for “top students”?
No. With focused prep and practice, motivated students at all levels can pass and earn credit.
How much can I save?
Each exam typically counts for 3 credits — often around $1,500 in tuition savings.
Which apps are free vs. paid?
Five iOS apps are free: US History I & II, Marketing, Natural Sciences, Humanities. Biology is premium.
